About me

Artist, developer,
creature enthusiast.

I'm Sasha De Silva - a multidisciplinary artist based in New York City with a soft spot for animals, puns, and the slow meditative act of making something by hand.

My work spans watercolor painting, digital illustration, and embroidery. Each medium asks something different of me - watercolor demands surrender, digital demands precision, and embroidery demands patience - but they all share the same goal: finding something true in the subject and bringing it out.

Animals are my recurring muse. I'm drawn to the way they carry emotion without self-consciousness - the dignity of a giraffe, the chaos of an otter, the absurdity of a pun-named penguin. I try to honor that in every piece.

Alongside the fine art practice I run Bearly Employed, an indie iOS game and app studio where I build and publish SwiftUI games on the App Store.
